About Ayurshop

Bringing authentic Ayurveda to every home with dedication, trust, and genuine care for your well-being.

Mrs. Ashish Anand

Founder & Visionary

Founded: 1st January 2026

Ayurshop is a dedicated Ayurvedic medicine and wellness delivery service founded by Mrs. Ashish Anand, an entrepreneur committed to making traditional Indian healthcare accessible to every household. The idea behind Ayurshop was simple yet powerful: people should not have to struggle to find genuine Ayurvedic medicines, travel long distances, or deal with unreliable local availability.

With years of experience understanding customer health needs and market gaps, Mrs. Anand recognized the increasing demand for trustworthy Ayurvedic solutions. Her practical experience in running local health and wellness operations gave her the foundation to launch Ayurshop with clarity and purpose. Over time, she developed a strong understanding of Ayurvedic remedies, common customer concerns, and the importance of authenticity in herbal medicine.

What sets Ayurshop apart is its unwavering commitment to quality and convenience. Every item is sourced from trusted and established Ayurvedic companies, ensuring that customers receive only the best and most effective products. Unlike traditional shops, Ayurshop focuses on fast home delivery, making the entire experience smooth, stress-free, and genuinely customer-centric.
